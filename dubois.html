STEP 04 · MATH TIME
int()
input() always gives you text. To do math, wrap it in int() to convert it to a real number.
HOW IT WORKS
wins = int(input("Wins: "))
Converts text "42" → number 42. Then: win_rate = wins / total * 100
⚠ WITHOUT int()
"5" + "3" = "53" — Python glues text together instead of adding. int() fixes this.
